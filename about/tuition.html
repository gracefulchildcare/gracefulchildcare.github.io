Tuition

Tuition is paid monthly and due on the first day of each month (or first Monday if the first day of a month falls on the weekend). Tuition can be paid with cash or check. Checks should be made payable to Graceful Child Care, LLC. Unless prior arrangements have been made, there will be a $25 late fee charged for tuition paid after the 5th day of each month. The charge for checks returned NSF is $25. There is no credit given for absences due to illnesses or holidays.
