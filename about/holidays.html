Holidays

In 2019, Graceful Child Care will be closed on the following days:

Tuesday, January 1 New Year's Day
Monday, January 21 Martin Luther King Jr. Day
Monday, February 18 Presidents' Day
Monday, May 27 Memorial Day
Thursday, July 4 Independence Day
Monday, September 2 Labor Day
Monday, November 11 Veterans Day
Thursday, November 28 Thanksgiving
Friday, November 29 Day after Thanksgiving
Tuesday, December 24 Christmas Eve
Wednesday, December 25 Christmas Day

Note:

If a holiday falls on a Saturday, Graceful Child Care will be closed the preceding Friday.

If a holiday falls on a Sunday, Graceful Child Care will be closed the following Monday.


Emergency Closure Policy:

Graceful Child Care is strongly committed to remaining open as much as possible. However, in the event of severe weather and/or snow, GCC may close. In general, if Seattle Public Schools is closed, GCC will be closed as well. School closure announcements can be found on the Seattle Public Schools website (www.seattleschools.org) and on local TV stations such as KING 5 News, KIRO 7 News, and KOMO 4 News. If snow begins falling during the day with accumulation on sidewalks and streets, GCC will close and parents will be requested to pick up their child as soon as possible. In the case of serious emergencies, such as earthquakes, fires, storms, and power/water outages, parents will be notified, and children will be cared for until parents or emergency contacts arrive. GCC is open with regular hours on days when Seattle Public Schools has a late start. Tuition will not be refunded for any weather closures.
